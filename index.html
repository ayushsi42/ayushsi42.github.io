Publications

IPO: Your Language Model is Secretly a Preference Classifier

Preprint Under Review at ACL
  • IPO leverages generative large language models (LLMs) as preference classifiers, reducing the need for external human feedback or reward models in alignment tasks.
  • By using the model itself to evaluate multiple responses and optimize preferences through Direct Preference Optimization (DPO), IPO achieves performance comparable to state-of-the-art reward-based systems.
